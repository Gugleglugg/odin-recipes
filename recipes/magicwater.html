Magic Water

Description

The ultimate magic water recipe for all magic purposes. Works 100%. No Water Magificator required.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Magic
  • Herbs or other flavourings you like

Steps

  1. Pour water into a cauldron.
  2. Chant your favorite spell.
  3. Apply magic to the water.
  4. Add salt, pepper, herbs, or dried frog pills according to personal preference.
