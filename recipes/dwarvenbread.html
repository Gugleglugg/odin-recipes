Dwarven Bread

Description

Traditional Dwarven bread is great for going on hikes, packing lunches to work, and going to battle. Legend says that you can kill a dragon with a single well-baked loaf.

This recipe comes from the noble Dwarf Lord who shared his biggest secret here.

Ingredients

  • 250-300g Plain Flour
  • 1 teaspoon White Pepper
  • 1 tablespoon Mustard
  • Half a teaspoon Salt
  • 1 Weatabix (crushed)
  • 1 lump of Butter
  • 250ml Bass Ale

Steps

  1. Mix the flour, salt and pepper together in a bowl.
  2. Add the butter and mustard and chase around the bowl until annoyed.
  3. Pour in the beer and fold together until the dough is a big lump on the end of the spoon.
  4. Knead by hand (or hammer) adding more flour to stiffen it.
  5. Form a rough circular biscuit shape, then push out onto a baking tray.
  6. Bake for quite a long time at low heat (150 on my oven), then remove after an hour and engrave a design and soak in a bowl of beer.
  7. Bake for another hour on the same heat. Leave to cool overnight.
  8. Soak in more beer and then bake again for a further hour or so on the same low heat.
